THE CRISIS

A System Under Fire

Battery fires are escalating across homes, waste facilities, and recycling centers. Current collection systems are failing consumers, retailers, and municipalities alike.

FIRE RISK

Home & Facility Fires Rising

Improper battery storage is causing devastating fires. New York City alone saw a 700% increase in battery fires from 2019 to 2022, with over 800 incidents since then resulting in 30 deaths and 400+ injuries.

~18 fires per recycling facility per year (up 41% in 5 years)
DISPOSAL

Inconvenient Collection Systems

Existing solutions require special trips, strict sorting, and consumer education that simply doesn't happen. Retail take-back is limited to business hours. Municipal drop-offs are infrequent and distant.

70% of consumers have no concerns about lithium-ion battery risks
WARNING

Fragmented Responsibility

Battery manufacturers fund compliance but can't control disposal behavior. Retailers sell batteries but don't manage end-of-life. Municipalities bear the fire risk and cleanup costs.

$0 owned physical interface between consumers and safe disposal
GROWTH

Growing Market, Growing Risk

The global lithium-ion battery market is exploding from ~$195B (2025) to ~$426B by 2033. With rapid device proliferation, improper disposal isn't slowing down—it's accelerating.

20.9% annual growth rate in North America alone
OUR SOLUTION

The PowerLoop System

A network of Battery Nodes placed at high-traffic retail locations, combining safe drop-off and on-demand battery purchase in one fire-safe, monitored unit.

1

Behavioral Alignment

Capture the battery replacement moment. Users drop off dead batteries and purchase new ones in a single convenient transaction - no special trips required.

2

Safety by Design

Steel enclosures, fire-mitigating media (vermiculite), temperature/smoke sensors, and limited internal volume ensure lithium batteries are managed safely - even when consumers misuse the system.

3

Data-Driven Operations

Remote monitoring tracks fill levels, temperature anomalies, and PM2.5 air quality. Predictive servicing prevents overflows and hazards before they occur.

4

Compliance Infrastructure

Certified downstream partners handle sorting, transport, and recycling. Retailers get compliance credit, battery brands get ESG visibility, and stewardship organizations get collection data.

ABOUT POWERLOOP

Who We Are

PowerLoop was born from a personal experience: a household battery fire caused by improper storage. That moment revealed a massive gap in our infrastructure - we've upgraded how we power our lives, but not how we manage the risk.

We're rethinking battery lifecycle management from the ground up. Our mission is to build the missing physical layer between consumers and safe battery disposal.

Power in your hands, safety in ours.
